About TrackCat tool

TrackCat is a tool for tracking the progress of the kittens (and cat{s,z}) in our CodeCatz meetup group.

The objective of CodeCatz is to assist women to enter in to the world of coding and change the world.

The TrackCat tool helps to track the CodeCatz projects and assigned tasks.

We are always open for a new potential members, so come over to say hi, see how we work. Especially welcome if you're a female kitten, who wants to learn more about programming or an expert cat, who would like to help kittens and learn new things from other Catz. You can use your GitHub account to login and get a New Kitten status. Speak with one of the Mentors, to become an Active CodeCatz member.

We code together every Wednesday at RAMPA Laboratorij, Kersnikova ulica 4, Ljubljana at 17:30.
